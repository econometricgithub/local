Analysing the Econometric Models for the Impact of Social Networking Sites Usage on Expressive Political Participation in Myanmar

Abstract

Social Networking Sites play an important role in people's daily lives.

  • In Myanmar, with the development of the internet, the use of social networking sites has increased to about half of the population.
  • The impact of social networking sites extends across a wide range of areas.
  • It can be found especially in politics.
  • Our research examines social networking sites usage and its political implications.
  • The main objective of our search is to investigate the relationship between social media usage and online and offline expressive political participation.

Econometric
